Common Collector Questions

How often should my mechanical watch be serviced?

We recommend a full overhaul every 5 to 7 years. Even if the watch is keeping perfect time, the micro-lubricants inside the caliber will eventually dry out, causing accelerated friction and wear on the gear train.

Do you polish the case during an overhaul?

Case and bracelet refinishing is highly subjective. We do not automatically polish cases, as many collectors prefer to retain the original patina and unpolished sharp geometries. We will only perform factory-spec lapping upon your explicit request.

Can you source authentic vintage parts?

Yes. As a certified atelier, we have access to original Swiss parts accounts. For obsolete calibers from the 1920s-1950s where parts no longer exist, our craftsmen will mill and turn replacement components from raw brass and steel in-house.
