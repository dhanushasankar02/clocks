Specialized Restoration Services

Grandfather Clocks

Complete movement overhaul, weight & chain repair, cabinet refinishing, and authentic moon dial calibration.

Mechanical Movements

Rebuilding vintage movements, gear cutting, bushing installation, and mainspring replacement with period accuracy.

Cuckoo & Wall Clocks

Pocket & Mantel Clocks

The Art of Revival: Our Process

Diagnosis & Consultation

We carefully assess the movement, case, and provenance, identifying issues with non-invasive techniques.

Precision Disassembly

Full disassembly, ultrasonic cleaning, and detailed inspection of each gear, pivot, and jewel.

Repair & Restoration

Hand-filing worn teeth, re-bushing plates, replacing springs, and restoring original finishes.

Calibration & Testing

Regulating timekeeping over 7+ days, oiling with vintage-spec lubricants, and sound testing the chime.
